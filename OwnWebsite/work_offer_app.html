Offer app design

The Brief

A mobile app design centred on the theme of offers/discounts from eateries/bars and linking this to the time remaining for the offers.

Outcome

Designing the mobile app to the iOS7 mobile design standard

Main menu, two-tab sub-menu, offer location, favourites, and the social media share menu

The 'offer time' screen listing the times of offers wasn't used but I felt it would have been of value as the app's main focus is the time remaining for offers

The wording for venue screens giving the offer/venue information was easily viewable by not being overlayed onto the offer background image

The User Journey

The Challenge

  • To accommodate quickly in the website design, the constantly changing form of this new start-up
  • The emphasis on offers/discounts linking this to the time remaining for the offers

Outline of my work

I designed a new Information Architecture (IA) to:
  • Highlight the app vision/ethos
  • Highlight the offers/discounts in a usable, as well as engaging manner
App design included:
  • An app design following relevant ISO guidelines and UX best practice, including Nielsen's usability heuristics
  • Research involved creating personas/scenarios and verifying/validating results by talking to the target group this app focuses on ie. spontaneous people, those aged under 30 years to ascertain their goals and tasks for the app, making sure the navigation was usable, easy to understand, accessible and organised in an understandable way
  • Observation and task analysis of paper prototypes to see how people can use the website design, real behaviour, A/B testing
  • App page 'mockups' for the functional specifications
  • Reviewed the apps YPlan, Yummly, Temptster, TopTable, Foodspotting, A Table, Yumtable, Bookatable, Hotel Tonight. The offer listings and the offer detail screen appearance was a result
